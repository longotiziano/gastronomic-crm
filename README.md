# ERP System for Gastronomic Business

## Context & Objective
After working as a administrative cashier in several restaurants, I noticed the lack of control in the data, including sales performance, waste of raw material and inefficiency in payments. So I decided to came up with a solution: a fully working app that allows you to upload your data and analyze it.

## Functionalities
There are plenty of functionalities to analyze and manage your restaurant!

### Reports
Analyze all your data with dynamic charts, allowing you to choose between:
- **Area**: Inventory, products, sales, finance, employees...
- **Date range**: daily, weekly, personalized...
- **Sector**: Bar, kitchen, general...

For more details about the charts, consider reading `docs/front/charts.md`.

### Inventory Managment
Allows you to upload your recipes, inventory and stock movements to the app. 

**Features**:
- Low-stock alerts
- Raw material waste calculations
- Automated beverages ordering recomendations

### Employees Administration
Manage your employees to avoid data messes and disorganization.

**Features**
- Enter and export employees schedules
- Manage their payments
- Organize employee's information
